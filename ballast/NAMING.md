# Ballast — Naming Options & Collision Notes

Status: **options for founder selection** (requested 2026-06-03). "Ballast" is the current working codename; the founder asked for alternatives before locking.

> ⚠️ These are **quick public web/App Store scans, not legal trademark clearance.** Before locking any name, run a USPTO TESS search and a proper domain/App Store check, ideally with counsel. Collision ratings below are directional.

## Brand brief the name must serve

Calm, defensive, preparedness-coded. Conveys steadiness against the unexpected and "a reserve you can lean on." **Not** cutesy, not a mascot, not alarmist, not gamified. Works as an app icon wordmark and a verb-free noun.

## Shortlist

| Name | Metaphor / fit | Collision scan | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Backstop** | A safety net / last line of defense; also a finance term ("backstop facility"). Strong defensive fit. | "Backstop Solutions" is a well-known **B2B** institutional investment platform (now ION) — different audience; consumer App Store namespace appears open. Common financial noun (harder to trademark broadly). | ✅ **Recommended** |
| **Holdfast** | A holdfast is the anchor a kelp/climber grips with; "hold fast" = stay steady under strain. Distinctive, ownable, preparedness-coded. | Used by some games/outdoor brands; consumer-finance app namespace appears clear. More ownable than common nouns. | ✅ **Recommended** |
| **Ballast** | The weight that keeps a vessel steady — the original working name. Excellent metaphor. | **Direct-ish:** an App Store finance app **"Ballast: Invest Wisely"** already exists; also Ballast Point (brewery), Ballast (HR/staffing). | ⚠️ Weaker |
| **Mainstay** | A mainstay is a stabilizing support (nautical stay); "the thing you rely on." Calm, supportive. | Crowded: Mainstay.io (residential real-estate intelligence), Mainstay Financial Services, Mainstay Capital Management. | ◐ Medium |
| **Bulwark** | A defensive wall/rampart. Strong defensive fit. | "The Bulwark" (prominent political-media brand + app) and Bulwark pest control. Brand-confusion risk; no finance collision. | ◐ Medium |
| **Bastion** | A stronghold. Calm, defensive. | **Direct:** `bastionwealth.app` is a live **consumer personal-finance / net-worth tracker**; plus Bastion Management (finance). | ⚠️ Weaker (avoid) |

## Recommendation

Lead candidates are **Backstop** and **Holdfast** — both nail the defensive/steady metaphor and have the cleanest *consumer-app* namespace. Between them:

- **Backstop** is instantly legible (everyone knows a backstop is a safety net) but is a common financial noun, so trademark protection would be narrower and there's a B2B namesake.
- **Holdfast** is more distinctive and ownable (better for trademark, domain, ASO uniqueness) at the cost of needing a half-second to "get."

If the founder wants maximum legibility: **Backstop**. If they want a more defensible, ownable brand: **Holdfast**.

## Round 2 — visualizable "readiness" names (founder steer 2026-06-03)

Founder steer: the name should be **something we can visualize to show readiness** — an icon can depict the prepared/covered state, not just an abstract finance word.

| Name | Icon / "readiness" visual | Collision scan | Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Reservoir** | A reserve **level filling toward full** — which *is* the product's coverage meter. The brand gesture and the core UI are identical: watch your reserve fill until "covered." | Only "Reservoir Financial" (an advisory firm); **no consumer app** found under the name. App Store namespace appears open. | ✅ **Recommended** |
| **Harbor** | A breakwater sheltering a home dot; "safe harbor before the storm." | Several uses (Harbor compliance, crypto, misc apps). Medium. | ◐ |
| **Aegis** | A shield with a filled gauge inside; "shielded and ready." | Security/insurance namesakes (Aegis). Medium. | ◐ |
| **Keel** | A weighted keel/ballast bulb under a hull; steadiness (sister to Ballast). | Crowded in consumer finance: Keel Wealth, keel.money, Keel Point Benefits. | ⚠️ |
| **Bedrock** | Solid strata under the house; foundation. | Very crowded: many "Bedrock Financial" firms + Amazon Bedrock. | ⚠️ |

**Round-2 recommendation: Reservoir.** It is the only candidate whose icon directly renders the product's readiness state, and it has the cleanest consumer-app namespace. Visual concepts rendered in `brand/name-concepts.html`.
