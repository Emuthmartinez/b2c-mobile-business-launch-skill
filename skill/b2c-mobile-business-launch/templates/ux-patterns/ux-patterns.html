Critical Pattern Inventory

Onboarding

Value first, meaningful questions, attribution in first third, plan summary, compliant review prompt, paywall, activation.

Paywall

Clear package choices, restore purchases, privacy/terms, loading prices, purchase error, entitlement success.

Settings

Subscription management, restore, support, privacy, terms, account deletion, auth recovery.

States

Loading, empty, error, offline, permission denied, premium locked, restored purchase, destructive confirm.

Mobile Onboarding Flow

1. PromiseProduct demo or clear value moment before asking questions.
2. QuestionsOnly ask what changes personalization, attribution, setup, or lifecycle messaging.
3. AttributionRequired source key plus other text, analytics, person property, backend persistence.
4. PlanReflect answers back and show the next unlocked value.
5. ReviewOnly after a real delight moment and through platform APIs.
6. PaywallValue-first purchase path with restore and legal links.
7. OfferTransparent dismissal offer or reverse trial when approved.
8. ActivateFirst useful task after purchase or before paywall when strategy requires it.

Bug Traps

  • Attribution screen exists but does not persist to analytics, person, and backend.
  • Paywall hides restore purchases or renewal terms.
  • Permission prompt appears before the user understands why.
  • Web purchase does not grant mobile entitlement.
  • HTML proof drifts from DESIGN.md tokens.
  • Android UX is marked ready from iOS-only references without Android-native verification.
