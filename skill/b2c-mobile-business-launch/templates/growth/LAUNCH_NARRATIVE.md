## Algorithm Compounding

The two types feed each other. A tentpole does not end when the views stop: a slice of that
reach keeps getting served your posts for months. That long tail is your **standing audience** —
you stop fighting a cold start every time, and weekly posts ride the wave of the big moment.

## Copy Guardrails

The 2026 do-not-do list (these are checked deterministically against the fenced post copy):

- No hashtags. They read as marketing and depress reach.
- No emojis carrying the message. Let the hook and the video do the work.
- No link in the main post. Put the link in the first reply so the post is not deprioritized.
- No generic "we shipped X" copy that earns an "okay" and a scroll. Make it lovable or hateable.
- Lead with a hook/POV, not a preamble. The first line is the whole game.
