## Voice

| Dimension | Rule | Example | Avoid |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Clarity | Say the result plainly before style. | Pending | clever but vague phrasing |
| Warmth | Reflect the user's goal without guilt. | Pending | shame, urgency, or pressure |
| Trust | Name limits and proof honestly. | Pending | fake scarcity, inflated outcomes |
| Momentum | Point to one next action. | Pending | stacked CTAs |
